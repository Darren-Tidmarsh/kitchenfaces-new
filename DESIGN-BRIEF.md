# KitchenFaces Website Redesign - Design Brief

**Created:** 2026-02-12
**Designer:** Henry (AI)
**Owner:** Darren

## Brand Positioning
- **Local kitchen makeover specialist** - Cannock, Norton Canes (WS11) & surrounding areas
- **Personal touch** - Darren runs it, not a faceless corporation
- **Value-focused** - Save thousands vs full refit
- **Speed** - Often completed in 1-2 days

## Competitors Analyzed
1. **Kitchen Magic** - Big national, 40 years, corporate but trustworthy
2. **Joe's Doors** - Friendly challenger, community-focused, 30k kitchens

## Our Differentiators
- Local family business (personal service)
- Speed: 1-2 days vs weeks
- Direct contact with Darren (not salespeople)
- No pushy sales, no hidden costs
- WS11 area specialist

## Design Direction
- **Modern & clean** (not corporate)
- **Warm colors** - Consider earth tones, sage green, or warm neutrals
- **Large photography** - Before/after is key
- **Mobile-first** - Most leads come from phones
- **Trust signals** - Reviews, guarantees, personal photos

## Proposed Color Palette
- Primary: Warm charcoal (#2D3436) or deep sage (#4A5D4C)
- Accent: Warm orange (#E17055) or golden (#FDCB6E)
- Background: Off-white (#FAFAFA) or warm cream
- Text: Dark gray for readability

## Page Structure
1. **Homepage** - Hero, value prop, services, before/after, testimonials, CTA
2. **Gallery** - Before/after showcase
3. **Door Styles** - Product catalog with images
4. **Worktops** - Options & colors
5. **Quote** - Lead capture form
6. **Brochure** - Free brochure request

## Key Messaging
- "Transform your kitchen in days, not weeks"
- "Save thousands on a full refit"
- "Your local WS11 kitchen specialist"
- "Free home survey, no obligation"
- "From £2,995"

## Technical
- Tailwind CSS (CDN for simplicity)
- Static HTML (no build tools needed)
- Form posts to 10nl.com/api/v1/leads
- Mobile-responsive
- Fast loading
